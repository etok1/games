Among Us is a party game of
teamwork and betrayal.

Crewmates work together to complete tasks before one or
more Impostors can kill everyone aboard.

How to Play

Play with 1-3 teammates in 3v3 matches or go solo in various game modes. Navigate dynamic maps, defeat opponents and complete objectives to claim victory. But watch out! Hidden among the Brawlers, one or more players might have a vendetta to take you down.
